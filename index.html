Minecraft Forensic Screensharing Guide

Advanced techniques for Java Edition 1.21+

Introduction

Welcome to the comprehensive forensic screensharing guide. This manual is designed for screensharers to effectively analyze and detect modifications, bypassing attempts, and hidden alt accounts in Minecraft Java Edition.

General Rules & Timeline
  • SS Timeline: Freeze suspect -> Ask for admission (screenshot) -> AnyDesk via Discord/Telegram -> SS -> Unfreeze/Ban.
  • Always record the entire screensharing session. Ensure they don't touch their mouse/keyboard.
  • Ensure Windows Defender and all antiviruses are disabled to prevent tools from being deleted.
  • Do not look through personal files (videos/images) without initial suspicion.
  • Respect the user, explain applications, and do not ban for joking admissions.
